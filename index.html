About Me

Hello! My name is Nikolas Rivera and I am a UX designer and Illustrator. I specialize in user research, user interface, design-thinking, graphic design and illustration. My Skills

User Research

User Empathy

In order to understand my users I must know who they are and what they need. I search for who my user is by creating surveys, planning interviews, and creating proto/user personas.

Talking with users directly allows me to comprehend their needs. I use Miro and Figma to note key findings by creating affinity diagrams and feature-prioritzation matrixes. This then narrows down the decision-making on finding the solution to the problem.

User interviews are where I find the most information on figuring out the problem and the potential ideas for a solution. I try to make my interviews more into a friendly conversation and I am always open-minded to anyone’s opinion. I believe making the user comfortable comes first.
